Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use the Ball Orbit: Mind Flow mobile application (“App” or “Service”). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you confirm you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Policy. We may update this document periodically and recommend you review it regularly.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only data necessary to operate, improve, and personalize the Service:

  • Technical & Device Data: IP address, operating system (iOS/Android), device model, manufacturer, screen resolution, system language, time zone, network type (Wi‑Fi / cellular), session start/end times, App version, installation source, and unique advertising identifiers (IDFA for iOS, GAID for Android).
  • Gameplay & Usage Data: Records of level attempts, orbit actions, completion time, success rate, hint usage, ad interactions, session length, and feature engagement. Used to optimize difficulty, stability, and relaxing experience.
  • Error & Performance Logs: Automatic reports generated during crashes, freezes, or loading failures to diagnose technical issues.
  • Voluntary Account Information: If you log in using Apple ID, Google, or Facebook, we receive your email address, display name, and profile picture. Used for account verification, cross-device progress sync, and support communication.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain core ball orbit gameplay, progress saving, and achievement tracking.
  • Optimize performance, fix bugs, reduce crashes, and improve interface layout.
  • Deliver relevant and non-intrusive advertisements through trusted partners.
  • Respond to support inquiries and resolve user issues.
  • Send important updates about the App, Terms of Service, or Privacy Policy.
  • Detect fraud, cheating, or abuse of in-app systems and reward mechanisms.
  • Analyze user behavior to develop new levels and relaxing features.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information. We share data only in limited situations:

  • With authorized service providers (analytics, ad networks, cloud storage) bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • With ad partners to enable personalized advertising (using IDFA / GAID only).
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or government request.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with the successor entity bound by this Policy.
  • In anonymized, aggregated form that cannot identify any individual user.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You may request permanent deletion of your account and data at any time:

While we maintain high security standards, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.
