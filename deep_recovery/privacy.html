1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the Service:

  • Technical & Device Information: Device model, operating system version, IP address, device language, screen resolution, network status, crash logs, app version, and unique advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID).
  • Storage & File Scan Information: Information related to duplicate files, cache files, temporary files, large files, screenshots, downloaded files, and recovery scan results generated locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Features used, scan frequency, optimization actions, cleanup history, session duration, and interaction behavior used to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Optional Account Information: If you contact support or use optional account features, we may receive your email address and profile information.
  • Error & Diagnostic Logs: Automatic reports generated during crashes, freezes, or abnormal behavior to improve app stability and security.

2. How We Use Your Information
